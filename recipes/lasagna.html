Nicki's Lasagna Recipe

The creamieat lasagna ever

This is a delicious and simple lasagna recipe, the ingredients are very easy to find and the best thing is that you don't need to be an expert to make it right!

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Seasonings
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the tomato sauce, seasonings, sugar and simmer.
  2. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe or: lasagna sheet, cream cheese and beef. Keep adding layers until you have reached a desired size
  3. Add more ragu on top followed by mozzarella cheese
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
